Welcome to Jackalope Science, Inc!

Jackalope Science is a 501(c)(3) non-profit group promoting and facilitating
Science, Techonology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics (STEAM)
in Tucson, Arizona, since 2013.

Jackalope Science recognizes the need for a paradigm shift in today's culture. Our vision is to confront society's problems by empowering our youth with an understanding of STEAM to address the challenges of the future.
